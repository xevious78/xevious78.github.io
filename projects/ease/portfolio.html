EASE

Smart Home Solution. (2015)

EASE is a smart home solution that allows each family member to easily schedule and control its devices based on the task that needs to be done.


Our team was made of three students. I created the User Interface in Sketch, and leaded my two teammates during the development phase. We implemented a working proof of concept with an iOS app and a node.js webserver.


This project was selected by 2015 Atos IT Challenge's Jury.
