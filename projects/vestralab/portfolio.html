Vestralab

Video for a Crowdfunding Website. (2013)

Vestralab is a french crowdfunding platform made for people living around the city of La Celle Saint Cloud, France. They asked me to make a video that explains the concept of crowdfunding.


The video editing was done with Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere. I also composed the music and did the voice over.


We showed the video in front of 50 people during the website opening.
