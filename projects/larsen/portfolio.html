Larsen

WebDesign for a Music Association. (2016)

Larsen is the biggest music association of my university. It allows more than 50 musicians to rehearse in dedicated rooms before performing on various stages across the city. I was asked to design the new website that would allows the members to get the latest news.


I used Sketch to design the User Interface, I then took advantage of Zeplin to share the design specifications with the development team.


The website is still in development phase.
