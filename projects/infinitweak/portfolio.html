InfiniTweak

Connected Pedal Board. (2016 - Present)

InfiniTweak is a connected Pedal Board that brings the world of Audio Plugins, widely used in recording studios, right on stage. It enables efficient live control over each audio parameters through intuitive physical and digital interfaces.


We are three working on this product. I implemented the first Proof of Concept on a x86 prototype board in C++ and Python, using WebSockets and REST API frameworks. I am now in charge of the UX/UI Design and of the Operating System optimization.
