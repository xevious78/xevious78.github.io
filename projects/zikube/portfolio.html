ZiKube

Musical Instrument for the Disabled. (2016)

ZiKube is a school project that enables people who cannot control their hands very well to play music in a brand new way. It gathers the brilliant interaction of the Theremin with the power of a live sequencer to provide a funny live jamming experience.


I created a Proof of Concept of this musical instrument with Arduino and Processing. You can read the full case study here.
