nD

Multidimensional Labyrinth Game. (2013)

nD is a new kind of challenging labyrinth game made for the iPad.


It is my first game made for iOS. I designed the game and implemented it in Cocoa/Objective-C all by myself. I also composed the music and the sound effects. My little brother was also involved in the level design process.
