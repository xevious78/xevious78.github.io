U-Tune

Digital Audio Workstation for Newbies. (2012)

U-Tune is a new type of Digital Audio Workstation designed so that casual and professional composers can quickly produce their next hit on the go.


This is my first iOS App. I designed the User Interface in Adobe After Effects, and implemented the whole software in Cocoa/Objective-C.


I made this app available on the App Store in 2012. It did not sell very well but I learnt a lot from this experience !
